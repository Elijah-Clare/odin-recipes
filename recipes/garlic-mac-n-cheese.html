Garlic Mac n Cheese

Description

Mac n Cheese is a dish that involves cooked macaroni noodles with a creamy cheese sauce. This dish add a garlicky twist to the flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 C of Uncooked Macaroni
  • 1 TBSP Butter
  • 1 TBSP Minced Garlic
  • 1 1/4 C Milk
  • 2/3 C Cream Cheese
  • 1 TSP Salt
  • 1/2 TSP Black Pepper
  • 1 TSP Smoked Paprika
  • 1/2 TSP Nutmeg
  • 1/2 C of Cheddar Cheese (or any personal preference)

Steps

  1. Boil

    Boil water in a large pot of salted water making sure there is enough room for macaroni to cook evenly. Add macaroni into the boiling water and stir immediately, cooking with the pot uncovered for about 8-10 minutes or until tender.

  2. Prepare

    Prepare cheese sauce by placing a TBSP of butter in a non-stick pan on medium low heat and cooking a large TBSP of minced garlic for 2 minutes till fragrant. Then add milk, cream cheese, spices. Mix this till completely smooth then lower the heat before adding the cheese. Stir till it melts.

  3. Mix

    After draining the macaroni, add the cooked macaroni to the pan with the cheese sauce. Mix and fold the macaroni into the cheese sauce until macaroni is fully coated.

  4. Serve

    Top with any customizations to your liking such as bacon bits or breadcrumbs and serve.
