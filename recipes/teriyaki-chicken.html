Teriyaki Chicken

Description

Teriyaki Chicken is a common Japanese dish where the chicken is broiled or grilled with a teriyaki style glaze often made of soy sauce, mirin, and sugar.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Chicken Breasts or Chicken Thighs
  • 2/3 C Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 C Honey
  • 1 1/2 TBSP Rice Vinegar
  • 1/4 C Water
  • 1/3 C of Canola Oil (or personal preference)
  • 2 TBSP Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 TBSP Grated Ginger
  • Minced Garlic
  • 1/2 TSP Sesame Oil
  • Sesame Seeds

    • Optional

  • Crushed Red Pepper
  • Green Onions

Steps

  1. MARINADE

    Add the garlic, soy sauce, rice vinegar, water, onion powder, brown sugar, honey, oils, and ginger to a medium bowl and mix. Set aside about 1/3 C of the marinade for serving. Add the chicken to the bowl or transfer the chicken and marinade to a gallon ZipLoc bag and fully coat the chicken in the marinade. Refrigerate up to 24 hours, or at least 1 hour

  2. GRILL

    Grill over medium heat until the internal temperature reaches 170F (about 10 minutes). Alternatively, the chicken can be cooked at 400F for 12-15 minutes in an air fryer or 25-30 minutes in the oven. Give the chicken a flip halfway through.

  3. SERVE

    Top with remaining sauce, sesame seeds, and sliced green onions & red curshed pepper and serve.
